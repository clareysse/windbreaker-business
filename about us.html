About Us


Good day!, I am Ashley Peregrina, the owner of The WindBreaker Co. The idea in creating a windbreaker jacket business was born from a simple desire: to create a product that was both functional and stylish. I knew I could create something better, something that combined the best of both worlds.My goal was to design a windbreaker that was lightweight, breathable, and stylish enough to wear on any occasion. I wanted to create a jacket that people would love to wear, not just because it kept them warm and dry, but because it made them feel confident and stylish.
